How About the Functional Group of Acetone?

The functional group of Acetone is Ketones. Ketones are compounds that contain a carbonyl group in which the carbonyl carbon atom is bonded to other carbon atoms.The bonded groups can be any combination of alkyl or aromatic groups.

The Other Name of Acetone

Acetone is organic compounds containing a carbonyl group bonded to two hydrocarbon groups. Acetone has a carbonyl group, where carbon and oxygen are bonded to each other with a double bond and is known as the simplest ketone. Acetone, an organic compound is chemically described as CH3COCH3. Aceton also be called to as propanone, dimethylketone, 2-propanone, propan-2-one, and Î²-ketopropane.
